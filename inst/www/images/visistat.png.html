VisiStat: Visualization-driven, Interactive Statistical Analysis


What is VisiStat?

VisiStat is a tool that allows users to do statistical analysis by interacting with visualizations. It guides the user to select appropriate statistical analysis tasks based on her research questions. It also prevents common statistical analysis mistakes and promotes understanding by collocating appropriate visualizations with statistical analysis results. User study revealed that VisiStat allows users to solve their research questions and it assists users to perform statistical analysis tasks beyond their knowledge.

Online demo: https://public.opencpu.org/ocpu/github/krishna221/StatViz/www/

The Team

VisiStat is a master thesis by Krishna Subramanian. It is supervised by Chat Wacharamanotham, and Jan Borchers. It is funded in part by the German B-IT foundation.
